Cookies Policy

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit the Atmosify website. They help enhance your user experience by remembering your preferences.

2. Types of Cookies

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: Essential for the functioning of the website.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us analyze website usage to improve our services.
  • Personalization Cookies: Customize your experience based on your preferences.


    • 3. Managing Cookies

    You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. However, please note that certain features of the Site may be affected if cookies are disabled.
